Frequently asked questions

Haven't found what you're looking for? Contact us.

Can I switch plans or cancel anytime?
+
Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your account dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle with no hidden fees or penalties.
Do you have an executive plan for Enterprises?
+
Yes. Our Enterprise plan is fully customizable — from data retention policies and user seats to dedicated onboarding, SLA guarantees, and custom integrations. Contact our sales team to build the right package.
Do you provide post-purchase support?
+
All plans include email support. Professional plans add chat support. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated success manager and 24/7 priority assistance.
Does Obzera offer a free trial?
+
Yes — start a 14-day free trial on the Basic plan with no credit card required. You get full access to all Basic features so you can see the value before committing.
Which cloud providers does Obzera support?
+
Obzera natively supports AWS, Google Cloud Platform, and Microsoft Azure. Multi-cloud visibility is available on Professional and Enterprise plans.
